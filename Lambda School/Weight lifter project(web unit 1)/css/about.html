About Us

Weight Lifting Journal exists to inspire and empower athletes of all ages. Our mission is to bridge the information gap between professional and amateur athletes. We attempt to level the playing field and democratize sport. We believe that every athlete should have the information and tools they need to reach their potential.

We are the athlete's partner in achieving their athletic destiny. Whether it is to make the team, become a starter, earn a scholarship or go pro. We provide authentic training, nutrition, motivation, skills and gear advice from the best pro athletes and performance experts.

We work with schools and leagues. We help teams win. We train and motivate athletes. We educate parents, coaches and trainers. We make a difference.

We create content and provide tools, but we are more than a media company. We are brand.WE are an authority.We are a competitive edge.We are a movement.
